Our Story

At GemelisseJewel, we believe every piece of jewellery should reflect timeless beauty and exceptional craftsmanship. Our collections are thoughtfully designed using premium materials and meticulous attention to detail, creating elegant pieces that celebrate life's most meaningful moments.

To create beautifully crafted jewellery that inspires confidence, celebrates special occasions, and becomes a treasured part of every customer's journey.

Our Vision

Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026. Your trust matters to us — here is exactly how we handle your information.

Information Collection

We collect information you provide directly, such as your name, email, shipping address, and payment details when you place an order, create an account, or contact our concierge team.

Data Usage

Your information is used to process orders, personalize your shopping experience, send order updates, and — only with your consent — share news about new collections.

Cookies Policy

We use cookies to remember your cart, preferences, and login state, and to understand how visitors use our site so we can improve it. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

Security

All payment and personal data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We follow industry-standard security practices and never store full card numbers on our servers.

Third-Party Services

We work with trusted payment processors, shipping carriers, and analytics providers who only access the data necessary to perform their service, under strict confidentiality agreements.

User Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time, and may opt out of marketing communications with a single click.

Contact Information

Questions about this policy or your data can be directed to our privacy team at 42 Mercer Street, New York, NY 10012.
